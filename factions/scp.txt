<h2>INFORMATION:</h2> The Foundation was a major, if not the most important, organization tasked with containing and researching various anomalies. Their influence was global, with sites and fronts located practically everywhere, from the most dense urban center to the ice caps of Antarctica. The Foundation's mission, to Secure the threats, to Contain the anomalies, and to Protect humanity from the anomalous, has been one of the greatest pillars of keeping the Veil up and the world in blissful ignorance.

Even if that is true, they have gone into hiding, avoiding the wrath of those who they have failed, or desperately containing whatever horrors that have not escaped yet.

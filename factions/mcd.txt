<h2>INFORMATION:</h2> To some, the Broken Masquerade was a disaster, an awakening that people have been living among ungodly creatures, and on the constant brink of disastrous doom. Others, however, saw only opportunity and profit.

MC&D was an underground company, making business only with those who were aware of the truly anomalous nature of this world. Now that everyone knows, their customer base has expanded as well. Their goods and services were always luxurious, promising to satisfy any desire, both mundane and esoteric, regardless of legal or ethical constraints. All of course, at a cost...

Though the company operates on the surface of Redwood City, there are many lucrative curios and interests hidden in the underground tunnels and facilities, ones that the company would be more than willing to get their hands on. And if they are desperate enough, they may hire those willing to retrieve them...
